Ensaymada

A soft, fluffy, and cheesy Filipino pastry perfect for snacks or desserts.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • Grated cheese (for topping)
  • Butter and sugar (for glaze)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add egg yolks, softened butter, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Gradually add flour and knead until dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Divide the dough into portions, shape into rolls, and place on a greased tray.
  6. Let the rolls rise again for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Brush with melted butter, sprinkle with sugar, and top with grated cheese.
  9. Serve and enjoy!

Halo-Halo

A colorful Filipino dessert made with shaved ice, sweetened fruits, beans, and topped with leche flan and ube ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened banana (saba)
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 1/4 cup macapuno (sweetened coconut strips)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened purple yam (ube halaya)
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • 1 slice leche flan
  • Evaporated milk (for drizzling)

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened red beans, jackfruit, banana, nata de coco, and macapuno.
  2. Add a generous amount of shaved ice on top of the layered ingredients.
  3. Drizzle evaporated milk over the shaved ice.
  4. Top with a slice of leche flan and a scoop of ube ice cream.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Halo-Halo!

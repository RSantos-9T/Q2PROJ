Chicharon

A crispy and savory Filipino snack made from fried pork rinds, perfect for dipping in vinegar!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork skin
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • Water (for boiling)
  • Cooking oil (for frying)
  • Vinegar and chili (for dipping, optional)

Instructions

  1. Scrape off any excess fat from the pork skin and clean it thoroughly.
  2. Boil the pork skin in water with salt for 45 minutes or until tender.
  3. Drain the pork skin and cut it into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Let the pieces air dry or place them in the refrigerator overnight until completely dry.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the pork skin until crispy and golden brown.
  6. Drain excess oil on paper towels and sprinkle with ground black pepper.
  7. Serve with vinegar and chili dipping sauce. Enjoy!

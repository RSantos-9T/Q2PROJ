Pandesal

A classic Filipino bread roll that's soft, fluffy, and perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • Bread crumbs (for coating)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add egg yolks, softened butter, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Gradually add flour and knead until dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Divide the dough into portions, shape into balls, and coat each with breadcrumbs.
  6. Place the dough balls on a baking tray and let them rise again for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!

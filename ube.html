Ube Halaya

A creamy, sweet, and vibrant Filipino dessert made from purple yam.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg purple yam (ube), boiled and grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Optional: grated cheese or latik (toasted coconut curds) for topping

Instructions

  1. Boil the purple yam until tender, peel off the skin, and grate finely.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the grated purple yam, condensed milk, evaporated milk, coconut milk, and sugar.
  4. Cook the mixture over medium-low heat, stirring constantly to avoid burning.
  5. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the pan (about 30–40 minutes).
  6. Transfer the mixture into a greased container or mold and let it cool completely.
  7. Top with grated cheese or latik if desired.
  8. Serve chilled or at room temperature and enjoy!
